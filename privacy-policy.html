Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Welcome to PlanScale. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

PlanScale is designed with a "Local First" approach, meaning most data stays on your device.

  • Camera & Photos: We use your camera to take measurements and photos. These images are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to export them in a report.
  • Microphone: We access the microphone only when you choose to record voice notes for your projects.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of reports generated, crash logs) to improve app stability and performance.
  • Account Information: If you sign up for a premium subscription via RevenueCat, we store an anonymous app user ID to manage your subscription status. We do not store your credit card information directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AR measurement and photo documentation features.
  • To generate PDF reports that you create.
  • To manage your subscription and premium features.
  • To improve the app through anonymous analytics.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

Your Data stays with you. We do not upload your project photos, floor plans, or measurements to our servers. They are stored in your device's internal database.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services:

  • RevenueCat: Handles subscription validation.
  • OpenAI (Optional): If you use the AI Analysis feature, the specific image you select is sent securely to OpenAI for processing and is not used to train their models (per their API policy).

4. Your Rights

You can delete all your local data by uninstalling the app or using the "Reset App" feature in Settings. You can manage your subscriptions directly through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
