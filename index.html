Privacy Policy for PlanScale

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

1. Introduction

PlanScale ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos, Images, and Project Data

We collect photos, annotations, tags, measurements, and project notes that you capture or upload within the app. This data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties unless you explicitly export or share it.

2.2 Location Data

We collect approximate and precise location data to:

  • Tag projects with location information
  • Automatically fill address fields
  • Enhance project organization

Location data is stored locally and included in reports only when you choose to export or share them.

2.3 Account Information (Email)

We collect your email address for authentication through Supabase (email OTP login). This is required to secure trial eligibility and subscription usage limits across reinstalls.

2.4 Subscription & Usage Data

We store subscription state and usage limits (e.g., monthly photo/report counts, trial status) in Supabase to prevent abuse and enforce limits. This data includes:

  • Trial start/end dates
  • Monthly usage counts
  • Subscription plan status
  • Credit balances

2.5 Purchase History

We collect purchase and subscription information through RevenueCat to manage in‑app purchases, including:

  • Subscription status
  • Purchase history
  • Device identifiers

This information is used solely for payment processing, subscription management, fraud prevention, and support.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Authenticate accounts and secure trial/limit enforcement
  • Process in‑app purchases and subscriptions
  • Generate project reports and documentation
  • Improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Photos and project data are stored locally on your device.
  • Account, subscription, and usage data are stored securely on Supabase.
  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • We implement industry‑standard security measures.

5. Third‑Party Services

5.1 Supabase

We use Supabase for authentication and subscription/usage state storage. Supabase may process:

  • Email address
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage and subscription status

5.2 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription and purchase management. RevenueCat may process:

  • Purchase history
  • Device identifiers
  • Subscription status

5.3 Google Play Services / Apple App Store

The app stores may collect data required for distribution and billing.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Supabase (authentication + subscription/usage enforcement)
  • RevenueCat (payment processing + subscription management)
  • Apple/Google app stores (required for billing & distribution)

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete local data via “Reset All Data”
  • Export your data via reports
  • Request account deletion by contacting us
  • Disable location access via device settings

8. Children’s Privacy

PlanScale is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Data Retention

Local project data remains until deleted or the app is uninstalled. Subscription/usage data is retained while your account is active or as required for compliance. Purchase history is retained by RevenueCat per their retention policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time by changing the Effective Date. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.
