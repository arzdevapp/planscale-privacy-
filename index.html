Privacy Policy for PlanScale

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

1. Introduction

PlanScale ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos and Images

We collect photos that you capture or upload within the app for project documentation purposes. These photos are stored locally on your device and are not shared with third parties unless you explicitly export them.

2.2 Location Data

We collect approximate and precise location data to:

  • Tag projects with location information
  • Automatically fill address fields
  • Enhance project organization

Location data is stored locally on your device and included in reports only when you choose to export them.

2.3 Purchase History

We collect purchase and subscription information through RevenueCat to manage your app subscriptions and in-app purchases. This information is used solely for:

  • Processing payments
  • Managing subscriptions
  • Preventing fraud
  • Providing customer support

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Generate project reports and documentation
  • Improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

All photos and project data are stored locally on your device using SQLite database and the device's file system. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription and payment management. RevenueCat may collect and process:

  • Purchase history
  • Device identifiers
  • Subscription status

For more information, please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Google Play Services

When using the Android version, Google Play Services may collect certain data. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

5.3 Apple App Store

When using the iOS version, Apple may collect certain data. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • RevenueCat (for payment processing only)
  • App store platforms (Apple/Google) as required for app distribution

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data using the "Reset All Data" feature in Settings
  • Export your data through the report generation feature
  • Request account deletion by contacting us
  • Opt-out of location tracking by denying location permissions

8. Children's Privacy

PlanScale is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device indefinitely until you:

  • Delete individual projects or photos
  • Use the "Reset All Data" feature
  • Uninstall the application

Purchase history is retained by RevenueCat according to their retention policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
